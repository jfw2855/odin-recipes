Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablesppon fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup water (120*)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teasppon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 cups pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix first 4 Ingredients.
  2. Mix water and oil; add to flour mixture.
  3. Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes.
  4. Place in a greased bowl; turned to a grease top.
  5. Punch down; place on 12 inch, greased pizza pan.
